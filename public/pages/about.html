About Blue Bean

Bluebean is a blog website where I share my experience with cafes and how they aligned with my studying

or working preferences. It is a site that will help people find the best study cafes based on their personal

preferences, whether that be how good the coffee is, how fast the wifi is, how much work space

there is, parking accessibility, etc. Fellow cafe enthusiasts can leave ratings and reviews

so that others can refer to them for better decision making.
